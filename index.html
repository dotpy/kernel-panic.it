Welcome to Kernel Panic!
This site is dedicated to Open Source and sharing of information, experience and software projects of any kind, because we believe that sharing of knowledge is the best way not to keep reinventing the wheel.

Our favourite topics are OpenBSD, Linux, security, programming and open source software. All feedback is greatly appreciated!
