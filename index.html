Welcome to Kernel Panic!
This website is dedicated to open-source software and sharing of information, experience and software projects of any kind, because we believe that sharing of knowledge is the best way not to keep reinventing the wheel.

Our favourite topics are OpenBSD and Linux system administration, security, e-mail standards and protocols, programming and open-source software. All feedback is greatly appreciated!

Contacts

Please feel free to send any feedback, contributions or suggestions to danix@kernel-panic.it or, if you think you found something useful on this website and you know how much caffeine is required to write good code or documentation, feel free to buy me a coffee through Paypal!
